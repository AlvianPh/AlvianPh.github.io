Overview

Jawir Apps is a Flutter-based mobile application designed to help users detect and recognize aksara through image scanning. The application focuses on delivering a modern, user-friendly interface while supporting features such as authentication, image scanning, and scan history management.

Problem

Many people experience difficulties in recognizing and identifying aksara correctly due to limited access to interactive and digital learning tools. Existing solutions are often not mobile-friendly or lack an intuitive user experience.

Solution

Jawir Apps offers a mobile-based solution that allows users to scan aksara images using their smartphone camera or gallery. The application presents scan results clearly and stores scan history, making it easier for users to learn and review aksara recognition results.
